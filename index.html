Why we should buy Unstitched Clothes ?

Unstitched Clothes was considered the purest form of clothing and in earlier times the technique of knotting was used while wearing the cloth. It remains a very popular choice in India where it forms the primary part of many of our traditional garments. The Unstitched Clothes are those which are used as a raw material for the stitching.

Unstitched Clothes are all about customizing the designs, patterns, and fittings to increase your chances of getting the perfect fit the first time. It can be difficult to find the right fit and pattern in pre-made clothes, but unstitched clothes allow us to get perfect fit. Even today a number of unstitched fabrics are used by both men and women.Most commonly Saree, Dhoti, Kurta, Lungi, Turban, Dupatta, etc. are unstitched fabrics used by men and women.
