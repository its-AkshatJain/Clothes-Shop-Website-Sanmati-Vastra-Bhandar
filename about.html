About Us

Welcome to Sanmati Vastra Bhandar, a trusted store for unstitched clothes and sarees that represent timeless elegance. Our shop has been around for over 60 years, carrying on the legacy of our founder, Shree Inderchand Jain, under the leadership of Mr. Dinesh Kumar Jain.

At Sanmati Vastra Bhandar, we carefully select clothes and sarees that are made with great skill and have a rich cultural background. Each piece is crafted with attention to detail and reflects the traditions that make these garments special.

We have earned the trust of our customers by providing high-quality products and personalized service. We value the relationships we have built over the years and appreciate the trust our customers have placed in us.

Under Mr. Dinesh Kumar Jain's guidance, we continue to provide a trustworthy and enjoyable shopping experience. We focus on quality, authenticity, and paying attention to the little things that matter to our customers.

Discover the timeless charm of unstitched clothes and sarees as you explore our collection. We invite you to find the perfect outfit that matches your style and personality.

Thank you for choosing Sanmati Vastra Bhandar, where the legacy of Shree Inderchand Jain and the trusted leadership of Mr. Dinesh Kumar Jain come together to offer you an exceptional shopping experience.

Tribute to Owner

In Memory of Shree Inderchand Jain (August 18, 1944 - June 1, 2022) and our beloved Shreemati Sakuntala Jain (29 July, 19XX - July 15, 2022)

Welcome to Sanmati Vastra Bhandar, a special place for unstitched clothes and sarees that showcase timeless beauty. Our shop is a tribute to my dear grandfather, Shree Inderchand Jain.

At Sanmati Vastra Bhandar, we celebrate the artistry and cultural importance of unstitched clothes and sarees. Each piece in our collection reflects skilled craftsmanship, intricate details, and rich heritage.

Shree Inderchand Jain had a remarkable eye for beautiful textiles. He curated a collection of unstitched clothes and sarees that capture timeless beauty.

Today, the management of Sanmati Vastra Bhandar has been entrusted to my father. With his expertise and dedication, we continue to provide exceptional service and uphold the values that have earned the trust of our customers.

We bid farewell to our beloved Shree Inderchand Jain on June 1, 2022, and shortly after, on July 15, 2022, our dear grandmother left us. Their love, values, and dedication to preserving our cultural heritage continue to inspire us. We carefully select each ensemble and saree in their honor.

Sanmati Vastra Bhandar is more than a shop; it is a testament to the trust and loyalty of our valued customers. We are grateful for the enduring relationships we have built over the years, and we strive to provide exceptional service and quality.

Explore our collection and experience the legacy and artistry that Shree Inderchand Jain and Shreemati Sakuntala Jain have passed down to us. Feel the craftsmanship, the luxurious feel of the fabrics, and the elegance of each design. We are honored to be part of your journey as you embrace the grace and sophistication of unstitched clothing and sarees, just like generations of customers who have trusted us.
